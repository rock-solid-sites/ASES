Historical research, state-of-the-art research, and synthesis should occur before tooling evaluation.

## Methodology Infrastructure vs Project Infrastructure
Knowledge architecture should support future projects using the methodology.

## Knowledge Architecture As Dependency
Knowledge architecture is research infrastructure rather than an implementation detail.

Relationships, provenance, and reasoning chains are expected to be more important than document storage.
