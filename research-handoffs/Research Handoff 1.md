## Most Important Discovery So Far

The strongest observed pattern may not be any individual tool, framework, or model.

Instead, the dominant pattern appears to be:

Progressive Externalization.

Observed workflow evolution:

Chat Memory
→ Session Notes
→ CLAUDE.md
→ Handoffs
→ Retrospectives
→ Issue Tracking
→ Crosslink
→ Multi-Agent Coordination

The working hypothesis is that project success increasingly depends on moving knowledge out of model memory and into persistent organizational systems.
