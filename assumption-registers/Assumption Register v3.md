# EDASES Assumption Register v3

## Classification Levels

### Verified

Supported by repeated observations and strong evidence across multiple contexts.

### Strong Evidence

Supported by substantial operational evidence but not yet considered conclusively established.

### Tested

Supported by limited direct evidence.

### Plausible

Reasonable hypothesis requiring further validation.

### Unknown

Insufficient evidence available.
