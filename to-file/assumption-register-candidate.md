# Assumption Register Candidate

## Title

Fresh-agent reconstruction is sufficient for autonomous project operation.

## Status

Challenged

## Origin

Implicit assumption identified during evaluation of Crosslink-based project onboarding and fresh-session orchestration experiments.

## Observation

A fresh model instance successfully reconstructed substantial portions of project architecture while simultaneously demonstrating semantic drift and overconfidence regarding operational readiness.

## Revised Assumption

Project knowledge resources may be sufficient for reconstruction of architectural understanding but may not be sufficient for reliable determination of operational readiness.

## Validation Question

Can an agent accurately determine when its reconstructed project model is sufficiently complete for autonomous action?

## Potential Consequence

If false, future architectures may require explicit project-state validation and confidence-gating mechanisms before autonomous execution is permitted.
